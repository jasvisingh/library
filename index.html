About Athenaeum Library

Serving students, faculty, and staff since 2001. We are committed to fostering a culture of learning, research, and intellectual curiosity on campus.

Our Mission

The Athenaeum Library strives to provide equitable access to a world-class collection of academic, scientific, and cultural resources. We believe every student deserves the tools needed to succeed.

Our dedicated librarians and staff are here to assist you with research, citations, digital resources, interlibrary loans, and much more.
