About Athenaeum Library

Serving students, faculty and staff since 2001 — we believe every learner deserves world-class access to knowledge.

Our Mission

Athenaeum exists to provide equitable access to academic, scientific, and cultural resources. Our team of dedicated librarians supports research, citations, digital access, and interlibrary loans.

We are committed to fostering intellectual curiosity and academic success for every student on campus.
